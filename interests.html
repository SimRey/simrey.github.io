Interests

A more personal side of Simone

“Lo qué uno quiere de verdad, es lo que está hecho para uno; entonces hay que tomarlo, o intentar: En eso se te puede ir la Vida, pero es una vida mucho mejor…”

- Mario Benedetti


Traveling is a big part of who I am. I absolutely love exploring Europe, especially Italy, which is like a second home to me. I enjoy getting lost in new places and meeting people from all over. Walking and running outdoors brings me so much peace and happiness, especially by the beach where I can listen to the waves and take in the beautiful views. Learning different languages is a real passion of mine because it helps me connect with others and understand different cultures better. Traveling constantly inspires me and reminds me of how amazing and diverse our world is.


When I'm stuck indoors, I'm all about football, especially the Premier League and La Liga. I also love diving into books, especially ones from Hispanoamerica; they really resonate with me and leave a lasting impact. And when I'm not watching games or reading, you'll find me geeking out over the latest in Machine Learning and Data Science. I'm constantly enrolling in courses and watching educational videos on YouTube to stay up-to-date with the newest tech trends.
